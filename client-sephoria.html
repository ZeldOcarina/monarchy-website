Developing digital strategies and innovative content for wider reach and more genuine engagement.

Sephora has been a leader in beauty and retail since 1998, with customers across 34 countries and over 2,600 stores worldwide. Known for their bold approach in terms of diversity, inclusivity and empowerment, Sephora continues their massive growth especially across digital platforms.

Monarchy’s Co-Founder and COO Andrea D’Agostini was Sephora’s Creative Director between 2011 and 2013, developing digital strategies for Sephora Italia to support and further boost their reach right in the middle of what could be the most pivotal era of the global digital revolution. Andrea developed social media activities that generated hundreds of thousands of views, comments and other forms of interaction. He also developed innovative digital platforms for Sephora’s content, including the brand’s BeauTV series on YouTube.

We developed innovative digital strategies for Sephora that empowered the target audience by engaging, relevant content.
